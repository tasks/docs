Tasks.org is a free and open-source task management app for Android. It supports CalDAV synchronization, recurring tasks, notifications, location-based reminders, custom filters, and much more.

## History

[Astrid Tasks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astrid_(application)) was a popular cross-platform productivity service. In 2013 Yahoo purchased Astrid, later announcing that the service would be discontinued. The source code from Astrid's open-source Android app serves as the basis for Tasks.org.

Tasks.org is not affiliated with Astrid or Yahoo.
