title: Email-to-task
---

Tasks.org Cloud subscribers can create tasks by sending an email. Forward emails to your Tasks.org address and they will automatically appear as new tasks.

## Your email address

Open `⚙ > App settings > Tasks.org` to find your private email address under the email-to-task section. Emails sent to this address will automatically be created as tasks.

## Usage

- The **subject line** becomes the task title
- The **email body** becomes the task description
- Forward emails from your inbox to quickly capture action items

## Options

- **Regenerate address** - Generate a new email address if you need to change it
- **Destination list** - Choose which list new tasks are created in (available after receiving your first email)
